Who we are

Lots of Lands is an immersive NFT-based game that is set in what we call meta-earth, a digital version of the real-world map. In this meta-reality players accumulate resources, territories and assets while interacting and building governance and business structures with each other. At its heart, Lots of Lands is all about freedom and autonomy.

Abundant Land Supply

1.9 million unique plots of virtual lands

Lots of Land is a virtual world where users can buy, sell, collect, and curate over 64 billion unique plots of virtual land. The Lots of land virtual real estate platform is mapped over the entire surface of the globe,

Fully Immersive game

How it works

Players are presented with a fun and compelling gaming experience in which they are able to pursue whatever paths of development and action they want. Players goals will not always align with each other, which is something that will have to factor in players strategies.

Unique Offerings

Better earnings for players

Thanks to the technological underpinnings of the game, the results of actions taken have real economic consequences.
Rather than the play-to-earn model that most blockchain games are built upon, Lots of Lands is built on a play-and-earn model, where neither aspect takes precedence over the other.
